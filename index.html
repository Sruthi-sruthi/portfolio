For a business such as hospitality, the front office department comes with an aspect of elevating customer experience with the business. Front Office department is a common link between the customers and the business. Front office staff handles the transactions between the firm and its customers. The staff receives the guests, handles their requests, and strikes the first impression about the firm into their minds.
