intoduction

Every multi-departmental physical business needs to have a front office or reception to receive the visitors. Front Office Department is the face and as well as the voice of a business. For a business such as hospitality, the front office department comes with an aspect of elevating customer experience with the business. Front Office department is a common link between the customers and the business. Front office staff handles the transactions between the firm and its customers. The staff receives the guests, handles their requests, and strikes the first impression about the firm into their minds. So we introducing a smart front office management system with face recognition. A facial recognition system is a technology capable of identifying or verifying a person from a digital image or a video frame from a video source .this system also provides message alert system to the front office manager when someone come to the firm ,it more help for managing front office and it make more impression and satisfaction for customers.

EXISTING SYSTEM

The front office information system includes mainly the property management system. Currently front office management system faces some difficulties in customer management. When new customer appear in front office sometime manager is unaware about new customer. It leads to creating negative feedback about firm. In Existing there is no alert system and face recognizing attendance marking in any firm.

PROPOSED SYSTEM

Front Office department is a common link between the customers and the business. So we propose a smart front office management system with face recognition and this system also provides staff management system. It also provides covid care.
